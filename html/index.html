Benefits of E-Filing Your Income Tax Returns

Claim Tax Refund

If you have paid more income tax, you are eligible for a tax refund

Avoid Late Filing Fee

A delay in filing ITR makes you liable to pay a fee of up to Rs. 5,000

Carry forward Capital Losses

If you have incurred capital losses, you can carry forward it and settle in future

Obtaining Visa

Foreign consulates may ask you to furnish your income tax returns for obtaining Visa

Easy Loan Approval

The income tax return serves as an important document for loan approval

Avoid Tax Notices

Filing tax returns is a must to avoid late filing notices from the IT department
